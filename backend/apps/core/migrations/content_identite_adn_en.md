## 🌍 Paris Bachata Vibe Festival

The **Paris Bachata Vibe Festival** is a project built within **Capital of Fusion**.

This festival aims to be a showcase, but above all a space of expression for an identity born in Paris.  
A movement. A vision. A community apart.

Paris is a multicultural metropolis, rich in influences.  
Here, dance and art are living cultures.  
Our vision brings together and converges a multitude of worlds.

---

## 🇩🇴 A strong essence: Dominican culture

This project places **Dominican culture** at its heart, in all its authenticity:  
live concerts, carnival, collaborations with Parisian Dominican associations…

A real, respectful, living immersion.

---

## 🔥 Diversity of styles and communities

Paris is today a unique ground where coexist and evolve:  
**Sensual bachata**, traditional bachata, fusion, bachazouk, moderna, influence, role rotation, solo…

A richness carried by an active, committed community, with a high level and particularly developed musicality.

---

## 🎧 A complete artistic scene

**DJs** with strong identities,  
nights with varied atmospheres,  
programming designed as an **experience**.

---

## ⚔️ Underground DNA: the battle

**Capital of Fusion** is also an underground culture focused on challenge.

**Battles** are integral to our identity.  
They are a factor of legitimacy and have allowed many French dancers to emerge.

At the festival, discover an **All Star** edition with a raw format:

- Cypher  
- Rounds  
- 100% improvisation  
- Random tracks  
- Call-out system  

Here, anyone can call out: participant or judge.  
The energy is real. Nothing is scripted.
